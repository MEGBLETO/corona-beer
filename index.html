The World of Corona

Since 1925, Corona has been encouraging beer drinkers to take an all-inclusive getaway to a place where the pace is slower, the vibe more relaxed, and life’s simple pleasures all the more enjoyable. Our philosophy has been embraced around the world in over 150 countries, including here in Canada. We are the #1 premium import bottle in Canada and for good reason; our refreshing flavour and iconic clear bottle are the hallmark of any gathering.

Products

Corona Extra

It should be no surprise that Corona Extra is the most preferred premium import brand in Canada. Classic and authentic, recognized worldwide for its high quality, refreshing taste and image, it’s best served with a lime wedge for an unparalleled flavour of relaxation.

Corona Light

Corona Light is light bodied, dry and crisp. The same great refreshing taste you expect from Corona Extra, with a little less alcohol. And that means more freedom to enjoy your time in the sun. Whether a day at the beach or a night on the patio, Corona Light has you covered.
